Xiaomi 14 – Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor, this phone features a 50MP camera, a 6.36-inch LTPO AMOLED display, 90W wired and 50W wireless charging, and an IP68 water and dust resistance rating.

Xiaomi 14 Pro – Similar to the Xiaomi 14 but with enhanced features, including a larger screen and improved battery life. It also has advanced camera technology and up to 120W wired charging.

Xiaomi 14 Ultra – A photography-centric flagship with three 50MP rear cameras, the Ultra offers exceptional performance for photo enthusiasts, rivaling the best Android cameras on the market.

Xiaomi 13T Pro – A value-oriented flagship featuring a 144Hz AMOLED display, MediaTek Dimensity 9200+ chipset, 50MP Leica-branded cameras, and 120W fast charging.

Redmi K70 (expected as Poco F6) – This is a powerful flagship with a Snapdragon 8 Gen 3 processor and great value for money. It offers solid performance and a stylish design.
