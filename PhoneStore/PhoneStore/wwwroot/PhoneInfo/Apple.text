=> iPhone 16:

---------------------------------iPhone 16---------------------------------------
Display: 6.1-inch OLED, 2556 x 1179 pixels resolution, 60Hz refresh rate
Processor: A18 chipset
Cameras: 48MP main camera, 12MP ultrawide, 12MP front camera
RAM: 8GB
Storage options: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB
Charging: 45W wired, up to 25W wireless MagSafe charging
New features: Camera Control button, Action Button, vertical camera alignment for spatial video capture
Colors: Ultramarine, Teal, Pink, White, and Black

---------------------------------iPhone 16 Pro-----------------------------------
Display: 6.1-inch OLED, ProMotion with 120Hz refresh rate
Processor: A18 Pro chip
Cameras: 48MP main, 12MP ultrawide, 12MP telephoto
RAM: Likely 8GB+
Storage options: 128GB to 1TB
Battery life: Up to 29 hours of video playback (streamed)
Other features: Titanium frame, Action Button, USB-C port with USB 3.0 support, Wi-Fi 7

---------------------------------iPhone 16 Pro Max-------------------------------
Display: 6.7-inch OLED, ProMotion 120Hz
Processor: A18 Pro chip
Cameras: Similar to iPhone 16 Pro, but may feature enhanced zoom capabilities
Storage options: 128GB to 1TB
Battery life: Up to 33 hours of video playback
Other features: Similar to iPhone 16 Pro, with a larger form factor and battery

---------------------------------iPhone 16 Plus----------------------------------
Display: 6.7-inch OLED, 60Hz refresh rate
Processor: A18 chipset
Cameras: Same as iPhone 16
Battery: Larger battery than the standard iPhone 16, offering better battery life


=> iPhone 15:

---------------------------------iPhone 15---------------------------------------
Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED, 2556 x 1179 pixels, supports HDR10 and Dolby Vision.
Processor: Apple A16 Bionic (5-nm technology), 6-core CPU (2 high-performance and 4 efficiency cores), 5-core GPU.

Camera:
Main: Dual 48 MP (wide) + 12 MP (ultra-wide).
Front: 12 MP with 4K video recording and Portrait mode.

Storage: Options of 128 GB, 256 GB, 512 GB.
RAM: 6 GB.
Battery: 3,877 mAh, supports MagSafe and wireless charging.
5G Support: Yes.
Body Materials: Aluminum and glass (Ceramic Shield on the front).
Colors: Black, blue, yellow, pink, green.
Additional Features: Dynamic Island (interactive display area), USB-C port instead of Lightning.

---------------------------------iPhone 15 Pro---------------------------------------
Display: 6.1-inch Super Retina XDR OLED, 2556 x 1179 pixels, supports ProMotion (120 Hz), HDR10, Dolby Vision.
Processor: Apple A17 Pro (3-nm technology), 6-core CPU, 6-core GPU (with real-time ray tracing).

Camera:
Main: Triple 48 MP (wide) + 12 MP (ultra-wide) + 12 MP (telephoto with 3x optical zoom).
Front: 12 MP.

Storage: Options of 128 GB, 256 GB, 512 GB, 1 TB.
RAM: 8 GB.
Battery: 3,274 mAh, supports MagSafe and fast charging.
5G Support: Yes.
Body Materials: Titanium and glass (Ceramic Shield).
Colors: Black titanium, natural titanium, white titanium, blue titanium.
Additional Features: Dynamic Island, USB-C (with USB 3.0 speeds).
